Pricing questions

Is there a free trial?
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required to start.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes, for monthly plans. Upgrade instantly and we'll prorate the difference. Downgrade at any time and changes take effect at your next billing cycle.
What counts as a "lead" in the database?
Each lead is a company record you export from our database to your CRM. View unlimited companies, but your plan determines how many you can export each month. Unused leads don't roll over.
Do you offer discounts for new EOS Implementers®?
Contact us if you're in your first year as an EOS Implementer®. We offer special pricing to help new EOS Implementers® build their practice.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime. Annual plans can be canceled anytime and you'll retain access through the end of your billing period.
