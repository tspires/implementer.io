Questions about the CRM?

How is this different from Salesforce or HubSpot?
Generic CRMs require you to build custom integrations and workflows. Implementer.io CRM is pre-configured for EOS Implementers® with native integrations to Ninety.io, Strety, EOS One, and Bloom Growth. You see your clients' rocks, issues, and scorecards right in their contact record.
Can I import my existing contacts?
Yes. Import from CSV, or connect directly to your existing CRM. We'll deduplicate and enrich your contacts automatically.
Does it sync with my calendar?
Full two-way sync with Google Calendar and Outlook. Sessions, meetings, and follow-ups appear in both places automatically.
How does the EOS tool integration work?
Connect your clients' Ninety.io, Strety, EOS One, or Bloom Growth accounts. Their rocks, issues, to-dos, and scorecard data flows into your CRM automatically. One dashboard, all your clients.
Can I track deals and revenue?
Full pipeline management with customizable stages. Track deal value, probability, and expected close dates. See your revenue forecast at a glance.
Is my data secure?
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share client information between EOS Implementers®. SOC 2 compliance is in progress.
