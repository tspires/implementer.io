Questions about automations?

What can I automate with Implementer.io?
Session prep (pulling client data, creating agendas), follow-up emails, client health alerts, document generation (session summaries, reports), scheduling workflows, and custom triggers based on any event in the platform.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. Pre-built automations work out of the box. Custom automations use a visual builder—select triggers, conditions, and actions. No coding required.
How much time will I save?
Most EOS Implementers® save 10+ hours per week on administrative tasks. Session prep alone typically takes 30-45 minutes per client—our automation does it in seconds.
Can I customize the pre-built automations?
Every pre-built automation is fully customizable. Adjust triggers, modify templates, add conditions, or use them as starting points for your own workflows.
What triggers are available?
Time-based (daily, weekly, before sessions), event-based (new contact, deal stage change, rock status update), and condition-based (scorecard drops below threshold, issue count exceeds limit).
Does it integrate with my email and calendar?
Full integration with Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, and Outlook Calendar. Send automated emails, create calendar events, and sync data across all your tools.
